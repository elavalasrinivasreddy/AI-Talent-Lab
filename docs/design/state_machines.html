4. Ambiguity Audit

QuestionAnswer
Can TL cancel after pending_jd_approval?NO — must reject at review step
Can TL cancel after open?NO — terminal in Phase 1
Is hr_pickup a hire_request status?NO — tracked on position.picked_up_by
Does revision_cycle increment on HR withdraw?NO — only on reviewer rejection
Does bias reset on HR withdraw?YES
Can two admins review same hire_request simultaneously?NO — atomic CAS lock
What happens on 30-min TTL?Status reverts to submitted, lock columns nulled
Can admin take over?YES — after 10 min, same atomic CAS
Who can edit ATS config after open?org_head and dept_admin only
What if no reviewer exists?Hard-block submit. Show setup CTA.
