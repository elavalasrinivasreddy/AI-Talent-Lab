Role Overview

We're looking for a Senior Python Developer to join our Engineering team. You'll architect and build our core backend services, drive technical decisions, and help shape the future of our AI platform. This is a high-impact role — you'll be working directly with the founding team.

  • Mentor junior engineers and drive code quality through reviews

  • Deep expertise in FastAPI, async programming, and REST API design
  • Strong PostgreSQL experience — schema design, indexing, performance
  • Experience with Docker, container orchestration, CI/CD pipelines
  • AWS proficiency (ECS, RDS, S3, CloudWatch)
  • GraphQL or gRPC exposure is a strong plus
